Esperanto keyboard for iOS

Esperanta Klavaro lets you type Esperanto with ease. It includes all diacritics and it knows that you are typing Esperanto. Autocorrect won't disturb you anymore!

Privacy Policy

  • The keyboard does not collect your input.
  • The keyboard does not connect to the network.
  • You don't need to enable Full Access for the keyboard to function.

About

Esperanta Klavaro by Slava Shklyaev is based on Tasty Imitation Keyboard by Alexei Baboulevitch.
